Lasagna

To prepare, start by cooking ground beef and Italian sausage in a large skillet until browned. Add finely chopped onions, garlic, and a blend of crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce. Season with salt, pepper, basil, oregano, and a touch of sugar, then simmer the sauce for at least 30 minutes to develop deep flavors. While the sauce is simmering, prepare the béchamel by melting butter in a saucepan, stirring in flour to form a roux, and slowly whisking in milk until thickened. Season with a pinch of nutmeg, salt, and pepper.

To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by a generous spread of béchamel, a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella, and a handful of grated Parmesan cheese. Repeat these layers, finishing with a top layer of meat sauce and a final sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan. Cover with foil and bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly. Allow the lasagna to rest for 15 minutes before serving to ensure perfect slices.

Ingredients

For the Meat sauce

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes/li>
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each tomato paste
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Bechamel sauce

  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Lasagna Assembly:

  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook 1 pound of ground beef and 1/2 pound of Italian sausage until browned.
  3. Add 1 finely chopped onion and 2-3 minced garlic cloves, and sauté until softened.
  4. Stir in 1 can (28 ounces) of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans (6 ounces each) of tomato paste, 2 cans (15 ounces each) of tomato sauce, and 1/2 cup of water.
  5. Season with 2 tablespoons of sugar, 2 teaspoons of dried basil, 1 teaspoon of dried oregano, and salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Let the sauce simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Prepare the Bechamel Sauce
  1. Melt 4 tablespoons of butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour to form a roux.
  3. Gradually whisk in 4 cups of milk until the mixture thickens.
  4. Season with 1/4 teaspoon of nutmeg and salt and pepper to taste.
Cook the Lasagna Noodles:
  1. Cook 12 lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Drain and set aside.
Assemble the Lasagna:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  3. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  4. Spread a layer of béchamel sauce over the noodles.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese (about 4 cups) and grated Parmesan cheese (about 1 1/2 cups) over the béchamel.
  6. Repeat the layers (meat sauce, noodles, béchamel, mozzarella, and Parmesan) until all ingredients are used.
  7. Finish with a top layer of meat sauce and a final sprinkling of mozzarella and Parmesan.
Bake the Lasagna:
  1. Cover the baking dish with foil.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  3. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
Let it Rest:
  1. Allow the lasagna to rest for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
